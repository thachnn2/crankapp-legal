Delete Your Account

This page explains how to permanently delete your Bước Chân Theo Chúa account and all associated data.

What will be deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, avatar)
  • All check-in history
  • Points and point history
  • Leaderboard entries
  • Mission progress
  • Streak records
  • Achievements

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Bước Chân Theo Chúa on your device.
  2. Go to Profile tab.
  3. Tap Settings → Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
⚠️ This action is permanent and irreversible. All your data will be deleted immediately and cannot be recovered.
