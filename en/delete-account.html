Delete Account

Last updated June 30, 2026

This page explains how to permanently delete your Bước Theo Chân Chúa account and all associated data.

§ 1

What Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, email, avatar)
  • All check-in history
  • Points earned and points history
  • Leaderboard entries
  • Mission progress
  • Streak records
  • Achievements unlocked

Anonymized aggregate statistics (e.g., total check-ins per church) may be retained without any link to your identity.

§ 2

How to Delete Your Account In-App

You can delete your account directly from the app by following these steps:

  1. Open the Bước Theo Chân Chúa app on your device.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
⚠️ This action is permanent and irreversible. All your data will be deleted immediately and cannot be recovered.
§ 3

Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or experience any difficulties, you may request account deletion by emailing pcngocthach@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request and permanently delete your personal data within 30 days.

§ 4

Contact Support

Need further help? Reach us at pcngocthach@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 30 days.
